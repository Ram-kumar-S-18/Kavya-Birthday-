A story written in the stars...

There is a rare warmth in the way you view the world.

Kindness, not as a gesture, but as something quietly woven into you.

A quiet strength that makes everything around you brighter.

You carry grace through ordinary days and turn them into something softer.

You do not just exist in my life; you illuminate it.

Some people are remembered by moments. You become the light inside them.

Little pieces of time, glowing because they have you in them.

Some frames feel calm, like the world paused to notice you.

Every expression, every small light, carrying its own quiet story.

And then the memories gather into one bright, impossible thank you.

Happy Birthday, Kaviya

May this year meet you with wonder, peace, and every beautiful thing you quietly deserve.
